Dr. Brian B. Lojero, DMD

I have been advocating quality and comfortable dental procedure ever since, because I personally believe that dental procedures shouldn’t be as painful as what it has been purported to be, while maintaining the quality desired. My practice has been a conventional family dentistry type, as we haven’t engaged in social media promotions or other type of marketing vehicle. We are just letting our patient and their experience do the advertising for us, and as a result, 60-80% or our patients are referred patients. We do not emphasize our practice as more of a business type but maintains it to be of a profession, wherein our primary duty and goal is to help, heal and educate rather than to earn.

Dr. Ma. Victoria Vaste Mae Quijada-Lojero, DMD

Dr. Ma. Victoria Vaste Mae Quijada-Lojero is one of the two dentists working in Dental Art Care Clinic. She is the wife of Dr. Brian B. Lojero. She graduated from Southwestern University (Batch 2008) in Dentistry. She is from Bukidnon and currently lives in Cagayan De Oro.
